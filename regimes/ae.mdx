### VAT Rates

| Key | Name | Percents | Description |
| --- | ---- | -------- | ----------- |
| <code>zero</code> | Zero Rate | 0.0% | A VAT rate of 0% applicable to specific exports, designated areas, and essential services. |
| <code>standard</code> | Standard Rate | 5% | Applies to most goods and services unless specified otherwise. |
| <code>exempt</code> | Exempt |  | Certain goods and services, such as financial services and residential real estate, are exempt from VAT. |
